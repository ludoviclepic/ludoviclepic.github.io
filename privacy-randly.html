Privacy Policy for Randly

Last updated: May 14, 2026.

This Privacy Policy describes how Randly ("the App", "we", "us") handles your information. By using Randly, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Overview

Randly is a random number generator for iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. The App is designed to operate with minimal data collection.

2. Local Number Generation

All random numbers are generated entirely on your device. The App does not transmit, upload, or store your generated results on any external server.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

Randly does not collect, store, or share any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book data
  • Photos, files, or other device content
  • Health, fitness, or biometric data
  • Browsing or search history
  • User-generated content

4. Local Preferences

The App stores your language preference locally on your device using standard system storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

5. Advertising

Randly displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service operated by Google LLC. Ad personalization is disabled by default. Ad requests may involve network communication and may process:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., IDFA, IDFV)
  • Diagnostic and performance data
  • Advertising interaction data (e.g., impressions, taps)

For more information about how Google processes data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy:

  • https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. App Tracking Transparency

When required by the operating system, Randly will request your permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before allowing any tracking-related functionality. You may deny this request at any time, and the App will continue to function normally. If permission is denied, no tracking identifiers are shared with the advertising SDK.

7. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service integrated into the App is Google AdMob. We do not integrate analytics platforms, crash reporting tools, or any other third-party SDKs beyond the advertising SDK.

8. Children's Privacy

Randly does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
