Privacy Policy for Recall IQ

Effective date: May 4, 2026.

Recall IQ ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Recall IQ handles information when you use the app.

1. Information Stored on Your Device

Recall IQ stores certain app data locally on your device so the app can remember your preferences and progress, including:

  • Selected language
  • Selected game mode
  • Selected difficulty
  • Session settings such as starting numbers and reveal-time adjustment
  • Best score
  • Best level
  • Best digit span
  • Recent session history

This information is stored locally on your device and is used to provide app functionality and preserve your progress between launches.

2. Advertising

Recall IQ may display banner ads served by Google Mobile Ads (AdMob).

When ads are requested or displayed, Google and its partners may collect certain data automatically in accordance with their own privacy policies and applicable law. This may include information such as:

  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Advertising identifiers or similar identifiers
  • Diagnostic information
  • Ad performance and fraud-prevention information

We do not control how Google or its partners process data collected through their advertising services. For more information, please review Google's privacy information:

  • https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • https://support.google.com/admob

3. Consent and Privacy Choices

Recall IQ uses Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to present privacy and consent choices when required by applicable law or platform requirements.

Users can continue using the core gameplay while ad privacy checks are performed. Where available, users can reopen and manage ad privacy choices from within the app.

4. Tracking

Recall IQ does not require App Tracking Transparency permission in order to use the app.

At this time, the app does not gate gameplay behind advertising consent or tracking-related choices.

5. Analytics

Recall IQ does not currently use a separate analytics SDK for gameplay analytics beyond the advertising-related services described above.

6. Accounts and Personal Information

Recall IQ does not require you to create an account.

We do not directly collect personal information such as your name, postal address, or phone number through the app.

If you contact us by email, we will receive the information you include in your message, such as your email address and the contents of your request.

7. Data Retention

Locally stored progress data remains on your device until you remove the app or clear the data through device-level means, where applicable.

Data handled by Google through advertising or consent flows is subject to Google's own retention practices and policies.

8. Children's Privacy

Recall IQ is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

If you believe personal information from a child has been provided to us directly, contact us and we will review the request.

9. Your Choices

You may:

  • Stop using the app at any time
  • Delete the app to remove locally stored app data from your device
  • Use the in-app privacy options, when available, to manage ad-related privacy choices

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the "Effective date" above.
